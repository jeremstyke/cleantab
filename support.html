Does CleanTab collect my data?
No. CleanTab collects nothing and sends nothing to external servers. Everything runs locally in your browser.
Will cleaning delete my passwords?
Only if you explicitly enable that option. A safety confirmation always appears before deleting saved passwords.
Why does Chrome show a permission warning?
CleanTab needs access to browsing data to clean it. That's why Chrome shows a standard notice. It's normal for any cleaning extension. No data is ever collected.
How do I keep sessions on certain sites?
Add them to the whitelist (protected sites) in the options page, and their cookies won't be cleared.
Is CleanTab really free?
Yes, completely free.
